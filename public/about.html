Meet Our Psychologist

Dr. Pratibha Chandak

M.A., M.Phil.

Dr. Pratibha Chandak is a practicing psychologist with over 25 years of experience. Since 2021, she has been serving as a counselor at K K Wagh College of Engineering, Polytechnic, and Pharmacy, where she has supported more than 300 students and staff through challenging times. Dr. Chandak conducts seminars to raise awareness and provide guidance on various topics, including skill development, time management, mentoring, stress management, communication skills, CV writing, and mock interviews. In addition to counseling, she mentors students to enhance their career prospects and life paths.
